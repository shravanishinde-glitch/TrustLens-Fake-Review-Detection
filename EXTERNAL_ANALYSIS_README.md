# TrustLensAI - Business Review Intelligence

## Business-Only External Review Analysis Feature

**This feature is exclusively available to Business users** for comprehensive review intelligence and competitor monitoring.

### 🎯 Business Intelligence Dashboard

#### 📊 Executive Overview Metrics
- **Total Products**: Number of products in your portfolio
- **Verified Real Reviews**: Authenticated customer feedback
- **Detected Fake Reviews**: Identified suspicious content
- **Average Fake Review Rate**: Overall portfolio health indicator

#### 🚨 Risk Assessment & Alerts
- **High Risk Alerts**: Products with >30% fake review rates
- **Risk Level Indicators**: 🔴 High Risk, 🟡 Monitor, 🟢 Good
- **Automated Status Badges**: Visual risk assessment for each product

#### 📈 Product Intelligence Table
- **Per-Product Analytics**: Individual product performance
- **Real vs Fake Breakdown**: Detailed review composition
- **Fake Percentage Tracking**: Risk monitoring over time
- **Quick Analysis Actions**: One-click product review analysis

### 🔍 Advanced External Review Analysis

#### Analysis Types
1. **Single Review**: Analyze individual reviews from any source
2. **Competitor Analysis**: Monitor competitor review patterns
3. **Bulk Analysis**: Process multiple reviews for trends

#### Product Association
- **Link to Your Products**: Associate external reviews with your offerings
- **Performance Comparison**: Compare external sentiment with your reviews
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Track how competitors are perceived

#### AI-Powered Insights
- **Authenticity Prediction**: Real vs Fake classification
- **Confidence Scoring**: AI certainty levels (50-99%)
- **Trust Score Calculation**: Overall reliability assessment
- **Business Intelligence Alerts**: Actionable recommendations
